Sliding-Tile Puzzle

A sliding-tile puzzle. Upload an image file and specify some number of tiles, then click the Start button. The image will be broken into tiles, and those tiles will be placed in random order. Press the arrow keys to move the cursor. Press Enter to "pick up" or "drop" the tile under the cursor. While a tile is picked up, moving the cursor will swap it with the neighbor in the specified direction. The object is to get all of the tiles in order.
